Robert Perez

Hello, I'm Robert Perez!

I’m a developer and first-generation student at the University of Southern California majoring in Computer Science.

I have the most experience with C/C++ and have done a lot of work with different technologies including Flutter, PHP, and MySQL. I've worked extensively with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as with popular tools such as jQuery and Bootstrap. I also have a strong knowledge of software engineering principles and object-orientated programming with Java and Python.

Beyond my technical skills, I am deeply committed to supporting historically excluded communities and spreading the message that as members of underrepresented groups, we are not defined by the boundaries and stereotypes placed upon us. Through mentorship and teaching programming languages such as Python to schools in underprivileged communities of Los Angeles, I hope to promote diversity and inclusivity in the field and inspire future generations.

Feel free to connect with me or drop me a message at: robertperezalfredo@gmail.com

Thrive

Social-media platform dedicated to supporting individuals in their addiction recovery journey, particularly those struggling with alcoholism. A safe and supportive environment for users to share their experiences anonymously and find validation in community through shared struggles. Registered users can make posts, create replies, and keep track of their own posts, where they can edit and/or delete them as they wish. Implemented password encryption and input validation across the entire site.

Java-based web application to help users find the ideal space to study.
